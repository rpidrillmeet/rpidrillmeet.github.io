History

The Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute Drill Meet and Military Excellence Competition challenges every cadet and midshipman to compete against other universities in the North-East region. Our drill meet brings out the best in other universitities' Air Force ROTC detachments, Army ROTC battalions, and/or Naval ROTC units. We welcome all branches in order to provide an intese competitive environment that instills excellence and devotion. This year, our 35th drill meet, will continue to bring this environment. We challenge you to show up and strive for first in your respective events.
